# Target Brazil E-Commerce SQL Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Target Brazil's e-commerce operations using SQL and Google BigQuery. The dataset contains over 100,000 orders placed between 2016 and 2018 and includes information related to customers, orders, products, sellers, payments, reviews, and geolocation.

The objective of this analysis is to uncover business insights related to customer behavior, order trends, logistics performance, freight costs, delivery efficiency, and payment patterns, and to provide actionable recommendations based on the findings.

---

## Business Problem

Target Brazil wants to better understand its e-commerce performance and customer behavior across different regions. By analyzing operational and transactional data, the company can identify growth opportunities, improve logistics efficiency, optimize customer experience, and support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Findings

### Customer Reach

- Customers placed orders from 27 states and 4,119 cities.
- São Paulo (SP) had the highest concentration of customers.

### Business Growth

- Order volume increased significantly from 2017 to 2018.
- Order value increased by approximately 136% between 2017 and 2018 (Jan–Aug comparison).

### Customer Behavior

- Most purchases were made during the afternoon.
- Higher order volumes were observed between May and September.

### Logistics & Delivery

- Freight costs varied significantly across states.
- Delivery performance differed by region.
- Several states consistently delivered orders earlier than estimated.

### Payment Trends

- Credit card payments were the dominant payment method.
- Installment-based purchases played a significant role in customer transactions.

## Business Recommendations

### Optimize Logistics Operations

Leverage best-performing states as benchmarks and implement logistics improvements across regions with longer delivery times.

### Improve Freight Efficiency

Analyze high-freight-cost states and identify opportunities to optimize shipping routes and logistics partnerships.

### Regional Marketing Strategy

Focus marketing campaigns on high-value customer regions while expanding customer acquisition efforts in underrepresented states.

### Enhance Customer Experience

Reduce delivery delays, improve shipment visibility, and strengthen customer communication throughout the fulfillment process.

### Data-Driven Decision Making

Continuously monitor operational KPIs including delivery performance, freight costs, customer growth, and payment behavior to support strategic business decisions.
